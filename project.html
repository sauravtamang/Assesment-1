Claybrook Zoo App

Claybrook Zoo is a small, family-oriented zoo based in Northwest England. It was founded in 1965. I was one of the developers we developed an mobile application that enhances experience of visitors during their visit at the zoo.

Uber

Uber is a popular ride-hailing service that allows people to book rides through its app. It was founded in 2009 by Garrett Camp and Travis Kalanick and has since expanded to operate in cities around the world.The idea was to create a reliable and convenient way to get a taxi by simply using a smartphone app.
